The Agency for Digital Italy (Agenzia per lâ€™Italia Digitale) is a Government agency responsible for the implementation of the Italian Digital Agenda under the surveillance of the Presidency of the Council of Ministers. Since June 2012 the Agency has replaced the former e-Government agency (DigitPA), the Department for Digitization and Innovation and the Agency for the Promotion of Technology Innovation. The Agency supports public administrations in their effective use of ICT, improving quality of services and reducing costs.

AgID has broad competences in e-Government, information society and technology innovation, including such areas as new generation networks, security, open standards, digital literacy, open data, digital inclusion, smart communities and digital health and education.
